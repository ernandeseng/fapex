# **App Name**: Fapex Velocity

## Core Features:

- Service Showcase: Visually highlight the range of Fapex Express services: Motoboy Delivery, Box Trucks, Pickup Freight, and Executive Transport with detailed descriptions and engaging imagery.
- Real-time Contact: Integrated WhatsApp and click-to-call functionality for immediate customer service access, ensuring quick response to inquiries and service requests.
- Route optimization tool: Calculate the best routes to serve your requests faster.
- Service Quoting: Contact the service line through Whatsapp/call

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant orange (#FF8C42) to convey energy, speed, and reliability.
- Background color: Desaturated navy (#242F41) for a professional, trustworthy base.
- Accent color: Lighter analogous orange (#F97316) to highlight CTAs and interactive elements.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, bold presentation. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) to maintain clean readability.
- Sleek, custom icons with orange accents to visually represent services and features. Incorporate motion-inspired designs to reflect speed.
- Dynamic layout with diagonal lines and geometric shapes to enhance the speed-focused aesthetic. Utilize modern card layouts with hover transformations for interactivity.
- Subtle animations, such as scroll-reveal effects, hover effects, and loading spinners, to create an engaging and interactive user experience.
